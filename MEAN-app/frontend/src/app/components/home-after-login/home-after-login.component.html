About

This appliaction is designed for maintaining the employee information. It helps create an environment that encourages communication and keeps employees and managers working together to meet company goals. A positive relationship between employees and managers leads to better productivity, more efficiency, less conflict and better retention

Employee

It consists of all the employee in this application.This component is basically for the employees.

Admin-console

This component is only available for the Admin user

This component can be accessed by the admin and not the user. Admin can perform addition of new employee ,deletion of employee and even edit of existing employee.

Tasks

This consists of tasks alloted to each employee.User should mark the task completed or pending.
